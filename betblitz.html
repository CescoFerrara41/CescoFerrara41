Overview

BetBlitz is a fake sports gambling website designed to replicate the fantasy football experience with real-time betting. Users can create or join leagues with friends and compete to see who can make the most fake money through strategic bets. The platform integrates real gambling odds to provide an authentic betting experience while maintaining a risk-free, social environment perfect for friendly competition.

Key Features

  • League management system allowing users to create and join competitive leagues
  • Real-time betting interface with actual gambling odds
  • Fake currency system for tracking winnings and losses
  • Friend-based competition and leaderboards within leagues
  • User authentication and secure account management
  • Responsive design optimized for desktop and mobile viewing
  • Live odds updates and bet tracking
